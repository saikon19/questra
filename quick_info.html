Questra Calculator Formula
Weekly income formula:
[package price] * [average profit] * (1 – commission – insurance)

Average profit last 10 weeks: 0.05

Example: Indigo package (65610):

Insurance:
65610 * 0.05 * (1 – 0.07 – 0.1) = 2,722.815

Without insurance:
65610 * 0.05 * (1 – 0.07) = 3,050.865
